Featured Project

Aarolic - Healthcare for the Public

Revolutionizing healthcare access with AI-powered solutions

The Challenge

In highly populated countries, accessing healthcare has always been challenging, with long wait times, inefficient appointment systems, and fragmented processes creating barriers to quality care.

The Solution

Aarolic combines GenAI with healthcare management to create a streamlined, efficient platform that serves patients, healthcare providers, and doctors through dedicated interfaces.

Key Features

Patient Interface

  • GenAI-powered personalized pre-diagnosis
  • Smart doctor recommendations based on multiple factors
  • Secure appointment management with partial payment system
  • Consolidated health records and insurance integration
  • Real-time health history updates and medication reminders

Healthcare Provider Interface

  • Comprehensive doctor database management
  • Real-time appointment and activity tracking
  • AI-powered patient chart summaries
  • Advanced analytics and reporting dashboard

Doctor Interface

  • Intuitive dashboard and scheduling system
  • Detailed financial insights and analytics
  • AI-assisted e-prescription generation
  • Seamless insurance integration

Technical Implementation

GenAI Integration

Advanced AI models for generating pre-diagnoses and medical summaries

Security

End-to-end encryption with hash-based protection for medical records

Architecture

Microservices-based system for scalable and maintainable healthcare solutions

Results & Impact

Reduced Wait Times

Significant decrease in appointment no-shows through partial payment system

Improved Efficiency

Streamlined workflows and reduced manual charting time for healthcare providers

Enhanced Access

Easier access to healthcare services through integrated platform
